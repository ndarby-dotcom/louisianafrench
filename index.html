Thank you for visiting Le Garde-Savoir! We're currently working on putting together the site, gathering cultural materials, and compiling a hub of resources. If you're interested in learning more about the project's goals, or have ideas for what else it can offer, please reach out at darby@louisiana-french.org. In the meantime, this site merely serves as a skeleton for what's to come. The History Page is the most fleshed out and will soon have several chapters about the history of Louisiana French speakers.

This website is a collection of resources to learn about Louisiana French language and culture.

It includes scientifically-backed lessons for learning the language, cultural materials, and a living literature review/researc database.
