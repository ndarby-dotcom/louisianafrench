# Louisiana French

Louisiana French is an endangered variety of the French language spoken in South Louisiana, 
with roots in the Acadian diaspora of 18th-century Canada. This site is a growing resource 
hub for anyone interested in the language, its history, its literature, and its speakers.

## What You'll Find Here

- [History](history) — from Acadian Canada to the Louisiana bayous
- [Literature & Oral Tradition](literature) — folktales, poetry, and song
- [Music](music) — Cajun music and its relationship to Louisiana French
- [Audio & Video](audio-video) — recordings and comprehensible input
- [External Resources](resources) — books, archives, and organizations
- [About This Project](about) — who made this and why
