Don't know where to start? This section has all of our lessons divided up by language level. If you're new to the language, there's a beginner section for grasping the basics. Already familiar with the fundamentals? There's resources for fine tuning and mastering your grammar!

This section contains articles focused specifically on what's different between Louisiana French and Standard French. It focuses on building from your current knowledge.

Pronunciation

This section has information about the sounds of Louisiana French. It begins with an introduction to the International Phonetic Alphabet, its application to Louisiana French, and has tips, exercises, and examples to master your pronunciation of the dialect!

Grammar

In this section, you'll find a variety of topics about the mechanics of Louisiana French, from pronouns to verb conjugation, negation, forming questions, and more!

Vocabulary

Learn vocabulary in a range of organized topics. From the basics (colors, months, etc.) to more advanced ideas, this section has all the words you need to begin your Louisiana French journey!

Practice

Here, you'll find a variety of worksheets and exercises to practice various aspects of Louisiana French grammar. In addition to interactive, self-correcting practice, there are several drills and tips you can use to practice on your own!
